This release is the final review snapshot accompanying the revised manuscript.

The compact packet regenerates 16 publication outputs byte-for-byte from the
included curated derived data. It now also documents and enforces its test
boundary: 41 self-contained packet tests pass, while five unchanged checks
that require deliberately omitted full-project production inputs are retained,
marked and skipped rather than failing with missing-path errors. No scientific
result, fitted model, table or figure changed in this packaging correction.

The separate companion supplies all 12 author-generated annual hourly BEM
Parquet bundles (1,296 logical cases; 7,568,640 rows).

This is not an end-to-end raw-data reconstruction claim. The archive documents
the excluded EPWs, thermal-comfort observations, prepared TSV fitting table,
licensing boundaries and incomplete upstream provenance.
